## Core infrastructure and protocols: decentralized finance
- Ethereum dominates DeFi with over 50% of total value locked across its ecosystem.
- Layer-2 rollups like Arbitrum and Optimism reduce transaction costs significantly.
- Smart contracts execute financial logic autonomously without intermediaries or custodians.
- Solana offers high-throughput alternatives with sub-second finality for DeFi applications.
- Cross-chain bridges connect isolated blockchains but remain a frequent attack vector.
