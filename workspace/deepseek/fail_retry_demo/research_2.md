## DeFi primitives and products: decentralized finance
- Decentralized exchanges use automated market makers instead of traditional order books.
- Lending protocols let users supply assets for yield or borrow against collateral instantly.
- Stablecoins maintain dollar pegs through overcollateralization or algorithmic supply adjustments.
- Yield aggregators automatically compound rewards across multiple protocols for optimal returns.
- Liquid staking tokens represent staked assets while remaining usable across DeFi platforms.
