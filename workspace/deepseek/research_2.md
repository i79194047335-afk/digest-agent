## Execution Strategies & Tooling: Arbitrage on DEXs
- Arbitrage bots monitor mempool and on-chain price feeds in real time to detect exploitable price discrepancies.
- Flash loans enable capital-free arbitrage by borrowing, trading, and repaying within a single atomic transaction.
- Gas price bidding (priority fees) determines transaction ordering, so bots raise fees to front-run competitors.
- MEV infrastructure like Flashbots bundles lets bots submit private transaction bundles directly to block builders.
- Sandwich and backrun strategies are executed programmatically via smart contracts to capture value around large swaps.
