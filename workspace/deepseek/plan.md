## Digest plan: blockchain
1. Origins and core architecture — the double-spending problem, Bitcoin's whitepaper, and the structure of a decentralized ledger
2. Consensus mechanisms — Proof of Work, Proof of Stake, and how distributed networks agree on a single truthful state
3. Cryptographic foundations — hash functions, Merkle trees, public-key cryptography, and how they ensure immutability and security
4. Smart contracts and programmability — Ethereum's innovation, Turing-complete execution, gas, and the rise of decentralized applications
5. Scaling, governance, and real-world impact — layer-2 networks, forks, enterprise adoption, and the DeFi ecosystem reshaping finance
