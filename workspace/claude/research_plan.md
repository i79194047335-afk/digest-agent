## Research plan: Arbitrage on DEXs

### Reasoning
DEX arbitrage spans three distinct dimensions that together cover the topic fully without overlap: the fundamental strategies and mechanisms used to capture price discrepancies, the technical infrastructure required to execute these trades competitively, and the risks and market forces that determine profitability in practice.

### Angles
1. Arbitrage Mechanisms — types of DEX arbitrage strategies: atomic arbitrage within a single DEX via multi-hop paths, cross-exchange arbitrage between different DEXs, triangular arbitrage, and flash-loan-powered arbitrage.
2. Technical Execution Infrastructure — the bot architecture, mempool monitoring, gas optimization strategies, MEV and block-building dynamics, priority gas auctions, and transaction ordering tactics needed to win arbitrage opportunities.
3. Risk and Market Dynamics — competitive landscape of professional searchers and MEV extractors, impermanent loss, slippage, sandwich attack exposure, declining profit margins, capital requirements, and regulatory considerations.
