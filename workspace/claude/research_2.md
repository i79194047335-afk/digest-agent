## Technical Execution Infrastructure: Arbitrage on DEXs

- Mempool monitoring bots scan pending transactions continuously, simulating their impact to detect emergent arbitrage opportunities in real time.
- Priority gas auctions let searchers bid high gas fees to outrun competitors and secure first execution of profitable trades.
- Flashbots and private RPCs allow searchers to submit bundles directly to block builders, bypassing public mempool exposure.
- Proposer-builder separation splits block construction from validation, concentrating arbitrage inclusion power among specialized block builders.
- Smart contract optimization reduces gas overhead per arbitrage leg, improving profitability even when gross price spreads are thin.
