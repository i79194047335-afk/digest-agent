## Economic Model & Tokenomics: decentralized finance
- Governance tokens grant holders voting rights over protocol parameters, fee structures, and treasury allocations in a decentralized way.
- Protocol fees collected from trading, borrowing, or liquidations flow to liquidity providers and token holders as revenue sharing.
- Total Value Locked (TVL) measures the aggregate capital deposited in a protocol, serving as a key proxy for adoption and health.
- Liquidity mining incentivizes bootstrapping new protocols by rewarding early depositors with native tokens, often causing inflation and sell pressure.
- Token emission schedules must balance user acquisition via high initial rewards against long-term sustainability as inflation dilutes token holders.
