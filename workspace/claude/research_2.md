## Arbitrage Strategies & Execution Infrastructure: Arbitrage on DEXs
- DEX-DEX arbitrage buys on the cheaper pool and sells on the expensive one within a single atomic transaction.
- CEX-DEX arbitrage exploits the lag between a CEX price move and the on-chain DEX price update.
- Cross-chain arbitrage uses bridges or intent-based solvers to capture price gaps across different L1/L2 networks.
- Flash loans allow arbitrageurs to borrow millions of dollars uncollateralized, execute the full arb, and repay in one transaction.
- Transaction bundling via MEV relays (Flashbots, MEV-Boost) lets searchers submit atomic bundles that avoid public mempool exposure and revert costs.
