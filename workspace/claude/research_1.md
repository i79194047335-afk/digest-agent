## Arbitrage Mechanisms: Arbitrage on DEXs

- Atomic arbitrage executes buy and sell legs within a single transaction, reverting fully if profit is not captured.
- Cross-exchange arbitrage exploits price differences for the same token pair across separate DEXs in one atomic bundle.
- Triangular arbitrage cycles through three tokens on one DEX, profiting when the implied cross-rate diverges from market rates.
- Flash-loan arbitrage borrows capital within one transaction, enabling zero-capital capture of price gaps atomically.
- Multi-hop arbitrage routes through several intermediate tokens to capitalize on accumulated pricing inefficiencies across many pools.
