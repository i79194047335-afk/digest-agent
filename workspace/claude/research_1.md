## Core Protocols & Mechanics: decentralized finance
- Smart contracts are self-executing code on blockchains that automate financial transactions without intermediaries or counterparty trust.
- Automated Market Makers (AMMs) replace order books with liquidity pools governed by mathematical formulas like x*y=k.
- Lending protocols like Aave and Compound allow permissionless borrowing against collateral, with interest rates set algorithmically by supply/demand.
- Yield farming involves users depositing assets into protocols to earn token rewards, often compounding returns across multiple platforms.
- Liquidity pools aggregate user-deposited tokens to facilitate swaps, with providers earning a share of trading fees proportional to their stake.
