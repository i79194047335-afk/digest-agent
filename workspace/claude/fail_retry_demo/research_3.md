## Risks & Challenges: decentralized finance
- Smart contract bugs are DeFi's largest attack vector; hundreds of millions have been lost to audited protocols.
- Oracle manipulation allows attackers to feed false price data and drain collateralized lending pools.
- Impermanent loss erodes liquidity provider returns when token ratios shift significantly from initial deposit.
- Regulatory uncertainty around token classification and cross-border activity remains an unresolved systemic pressure.
- Money-lego composability creates contagion risk; one protocol failure can cascade across interconnected positions.
