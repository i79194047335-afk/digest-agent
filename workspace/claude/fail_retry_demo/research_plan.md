# Research Plan: Decentralized Finance

## Angles

### Angle 1: Core Protocols
Research the foundational building blocks of DeFi — automated market makers, lending protocols, stablecoins, and liquidity pools that form the substrate of the ecosystem.

### Angle 2: Governance & Tokenomics
Research how decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs) govern DeFi protocols, how token-based voting works, and how incentive structures (yield farming, liquidity mining) shape protocol behavior.

### Angle 3: Risks & Challenges
Research the principal risks in DeFi — smart contract exploits, oracle manipulation, regulatory uncertainty, impermanent loss, and systemic contagion across money legos.
