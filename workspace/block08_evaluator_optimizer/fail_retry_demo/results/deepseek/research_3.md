## Risks, regulation, and future outlook: decentralized finance
- Smart contract vulnerabilities have led to billions in stolen funds across major protocols.
- Regulatory frameworks remain fragmented with different approaches across the US, EU, and Asia.
- Oracle manipulation attacks exploit price feeds to trigger unfair liquidations and arbitrage.
- Governance token holders vote on protocol changes but face low participation and plutocracy risks.
- Institutional adoption is accelerating through tokenized real-world assets and compliant DeFi platforms.
