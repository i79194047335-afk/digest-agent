## Governance & Tokenomics: decentralized finance
- DAOs govern major DeFi protocols through on-chain proposals where token holders vote on parameter changes.
- Governance tokens grant voting rights and often carry economic value tied to protocol revenue or fee capture.
- Liquidity mining programs distribute governance tokens to users who provide capital, bootstrapping adoption rapidly.
- Vote delegation allows passive holders to assign their voting power to specialized, active delegates.
- Tokenomic design shapes long-term protocol health; poor emission schedules lead to mercenary capital exits.
