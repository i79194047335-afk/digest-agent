## Core Protocols: decentralized finance
- Automated market makers like Uniswap replace order books with constant-product liquidity pools.
- Lending protocols such as Aave and Compound allow permissionless borrowing against on-chain collateral.
- Stablecoins are pegged digital assets that anchor DeFi activity; they exist as fiat-backed, crypto-backed, or algorithmic variants.
- Liquidity providers deposit token pairs into pools and earn fees proportional to their share.
- Composability lets protocols interoperate trustlessly, enabling complex multi-step transactions in a single on-chain call.
