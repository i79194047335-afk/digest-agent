## Facts: decentralized finance

- The total value locked (TVL) across all DeFi protocols peaked at approximately $175 billion in November 2021, according to DeFi Llama tracking data.
- MakerDAO launched DAI, the first decentralized stablecoin on Ethereum, in December 2017, creating the foundational primitive for DeFi lending markets.
- Uniswap v1 launched on the Ethereum mainnet in November 2018, introducing the automated market maker (AMM) model that replaced traditional order books with liquidity pools.
- The DAO hack of June 2016 saw attackers drain roughly $60 million worth of ETH from the first decentralized autonomous organization, an event that shaped DeFi governance and security practices for years afterward.
- Ethereum's transition to Proof of Stake in September 2022 (the Merge) cut the network's energy consumption by approximately 99.95%, addressing one of the most persistent criticisms of DeFi's environmental footprint.
