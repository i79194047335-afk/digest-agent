## Facts: decentralized finance

- The total value locked (TVL) in DeFi protocols peaked at approximately $180 billion in November 2021 before retracting sharply through 2022.
- Uniswap, launched in November 2018, became the first major automated market maker (AMM) DEX and by 2021 was processing over $1 billion in daily trading volume.
- MakerDAO launched its DAI stablecoin in December 2017, making it one of the first decentralized stablecoins maintaining a soft peg to the US dollar via over-collateralized crypto assets.
- The Ethereum network hosts roughly 60–70% of all DeFi activity by TVL, with competing chains such as BNB Chain, Solana, and Arbitrum collectively accounting for most of the remainder as of 2024.
- Compound Finance introduced liquidity mining (yield farming) in June 2020, triggering the "DeFi Summer" that grew total TVL from under $1 billion to over $10 billion within a single year.
